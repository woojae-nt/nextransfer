Our Vision

We see a paradigm shift in finance brought on by the demands of the true stakeholders and constituents to serve the needs of the many, and not only the few.

As resonated in Abraham Lincoln’s speech in 1863 during the American Civil War,

Finance of the people, for the people, by the people.


Our Team

Our team collectively houses over three decades of diverse experience in finance, investing, marketing and tech across leading multinational companies and financial institutions. This exposure has served as the impetus for wanting to change the system from the inside. We have lived and worked in nearly 10 countries worldwide and have spent thousands of our own dollars on fees and other charges for cross-border money transferring. We have felt the pain of losing money firsthand. This makes us especially excited to liberate people from the old, inefficient, slow and highly expensive international financial system.
